Getting to Reigate

Reigate is just off the A217 and M25 (junction 8) for those of you who want to drive. There is ample parking at the Church and the Reception.

There is a direct train service from London Victoria to Reigate, running every 30 min, with more frequent services to nearby Redhill. The train takes around 40 min and the station is less than 10 min walk from both the church and reception. The last train back to London is at 11:55 pm (or  00:53 am from Redhill).

If you're travelling locally, try a local taxi firm:

Priory Cars: 01737 243333
Road Runners: 01737 760076
Bellfry Cars: 01737 766111
